    About Us

    MaProD - Mut, Mathematics and Programming for Development is a student tech enthusiast club at Murang'a University of Technology, with a goal of introducing student into tech and heping those in tech to improve and grow in the fielding with the latest skills in programming and Technology in General. At MaProD PEER LEARNING IS CORE.

    Our Mission

    To empower every student with interest in technology with every techinal guidance and mentorship.

    Our Plan

    To offer a friendly environment to peer learning and technical advancement in the school.

    Our Vision

    Being a techinological hub at Muranga University of technology is main goal and balancing gender inbalance in tech fields.
